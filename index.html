What is UWISage?

UWISage is an AI-powered assistant that helps UWI students plan, manage, and optimize their academic journey. It builds personalized study schedules, finds past papers, and more.

Smart Scheduler

Uses advanced algorithms (Simplex & CSP) to create study plans that work for your life.

Resource Finder

Quickly fetches ranked past papers and articles using the BM25 search algorithm.

Chat Interface

Interact naturally through a simple chat UI — just ask and get results instantly.

How Does It Work?

Sign up then login to UWISage.
Chat with Sage and request a study schedule.
Upload your preferences and let UWISage generate your plan.
Adjust & repeat if needed.
